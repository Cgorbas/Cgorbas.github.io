City Latitudes vs Maximum Temperatures


The first weather factor the team looked into was the Maximum Temperature. Our expectations were that the maximum temperatures would be more symmetrically distributed; however, we found that the temperatures tended to be much colder in the northern hemisphere. Eighty eight percent of the global population lives in the northern hemisphere; it is possible that this concentration has affected the temperature in these latitudes but this is not in the scope of this research project.
